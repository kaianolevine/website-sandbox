# Pricing

Duration: by default, lessons are 50 mins

Typical Rates:

Regular Rate: $70 USD

Discounted Rate: $50 USD


All bookings will be made through the booking portal, which requires a credit card payment entry. However, if you would prefer to pay in cash, use the code PAYINCASH at booking. Payment will be due at the beginning of the lesson meeting.

There is a single discounted rate for students and those on a fixed/limited income. At booking, use discount code DISCOUNTRATE through the booking portal.​​

# Common Questions

​Q: Do I need to bring a partner?

A: A partner is not required but it is highly encouraged to get the most out of the lesson time


Q: Can I split a private lesson with a partner?

A: You are welcome to split lessons with as many people as you would like. This includes the same rate for small group private lessons.
